Case Studies

How We Helped Our Clients Achieve Success

Acerta works with leading manufacturers across the globe. We are constantly in pursuit of better analysis of vehicle data and strive to deliver immediate impact and growing advantage to our clients across a range of issues.

The following case studies showcase some of our clients' success stories.

Engine Misfires

Acerta's Molecule™ accurately identified the specific conditions which led to previously undetected engine misfires and alerted the client of each occurrence.

Air Leak

Acerta's Fusion™ automatically identified an air leak in the exhaust system of a vehicle during a road test. The insight provided by Fusion™ reduced the root cause analysis from 2 weeks to 1 hour.

Bearing Failure

Acerta's Reaction™ provided Remaining Useful Life prediction for bearings, which allowed for robust and reliable predictive maintenance.
