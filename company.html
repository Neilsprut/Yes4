About Acerta

Acerta provides machine intelligence solutions that turn raw machine data into informed decisions.

Acerta Systems Analytics was founded by Greta Cutulenco and Sebastian Fischmeister and is headquartered in Waterloo, Canada. The company got its roots from research conducted at the Univerity of Waterloo. Acerta is currently part of the Velocity inclubator and Communitech.
